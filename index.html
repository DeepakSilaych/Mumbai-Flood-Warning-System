Who we are and What do we do

The "Mumbai Urban Flood Living Lab" is an innovative citizen-driven initiative that seamlessly integrates AI/ML-powered rainfall forecasting, real-time crowdsourced flood data, and advanced hydro-meteorological flood modelling. This project intends to provide accurate flood forecasting, and enable real-time flood monitoring in the city of Mumbai by making the citizens as primary stakeholders.
